The table below provides access to a summary report by year and by shower, and reports on the trajectories and orbits of matched events.

The Annual and Shower reports provide a summary of the number of meteors detected, their characteristics such as magnitude, velocity, direction of travel, and includes some statistics on the shower or whole year.

The Orbit reports provide detailed information about the trajectory and Orbit of any events that were detected on two or more cameras, and for which this analysis was therefore possible. On the orbit report pages you will find images of the event, graphics showing the orbit and trajectory and other information.
