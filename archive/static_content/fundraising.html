Fundraising

If you'd still like to help with data analysis costs, you can donate directly via our bank account. This has the advantage that we are not charged administration fees by JustGiving, and also we are not taxed on the donations as our income is below the tax threshold. As we're not a charity, Justgiving donations are treated as earnings of the person who sets up the fundraiser and are subject to tax at their marginal rate. However, funds paid directly into our account can be offset against costs.

If you're interested in donating via our bank account please contact markmcintyre99 at googlemail.com for further details.
